FAQ

Common Questions

How much mulch do I need?

We calculate this for you during the quote process. Generally, 1 cubic yard covers about 100 square feet at 3" depth.

What type of mulch do you use?

We primarily use premium double-shredded hardwood mulch. Color options include natural brown, black, and red.

Do I need to be home?

Not necessarily. As long as we can access the work areas, we can complete the job while you're away.

How do I pay?

We accept cash, check, Venmo, Zelle, and other digital payment methods. Payment is due upon completion.

What if it rains?

Light rain doesn't stop us. For heavy rain, we'll reschedule to ensure quality results. We always communicate any changes.

Are you insured?

Yes, we carry full liability insurance for your peace of mind and ours.
