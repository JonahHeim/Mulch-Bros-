Our Story

How We Got Started

James and Jack connected over a shared passion for landscaping and a drive for entrepreneurship. They founded Mulch Bros after recognizing a need for more positivity and quality people in the industry. Their mission is simple: to provide the 5-star experience at a fair price, all while sharing the joy and love of Jesus with every homeowner they serve.
